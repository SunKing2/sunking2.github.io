Just Your Fool - Cyndi Lauper. Album: Memphis Blues. June 2010.

Memphis Blues

Just Your Fool

[digg=http://digg.com/music/Cyndi_Lauper_Nobody_s_Fool_With_Upcoming_Memphis_Blues_album] Cyndi Lauper's, American pop icon of the 80's is releasing her eleventh studio album on June 22, 2010.

And the song "Just Your Fool" is a good introduction to the album. Cyndi's talent shows itself on this blues release, and I'm convinced at least this song will be around for awhile.

You can listen to the song here (if it's still up) at YouTube: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-Fp4_TT1Q_A

The album is on Amazon.com here too. It's already number 130 in music.

That's Charlie Musselwhite on harmonica, and he's steaming up the place.
