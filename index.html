Music in Recovery

Who, What, When, Where, Why, How?

  • An Open mike in Vanier. No mics (haha), no amplifiers, BYO acoustic instrument!
  • Running every Saturday since June 2017, including holidays 12:30pm to 3:30pm
  • 265 Montreal Road, Ottawa, side Entrance (across from Jean Coutu). Street parking only
  • People in Recovery need something to do!, and it's fun!
  • It's open to everyone, everyone is welcome, and lots come to watch!
  • Bus #12. (or Bus 9, 14 with 7 to 10 minute walk)
