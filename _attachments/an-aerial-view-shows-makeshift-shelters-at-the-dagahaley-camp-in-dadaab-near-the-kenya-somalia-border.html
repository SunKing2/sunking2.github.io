An aerial view shows makeshift shelters at the Dagahaley camp in Dadaab, near the Kenya-Somalia border, April 3, 2011. Drought and two decades of violence have forced Somalis to flee their country and seek refuge at the three Dadaab camps, which were originally designed to accommodate 90,000 people. Dadaab is the biggest refugee camp in the world, with more than 332,000 refugees mainly from Somalia. REUTERS/Thomas Mukoya (KENYA - Tags: SOCIETY CIVIL UNREST POLITICS IMAGES OF THE DAY)
